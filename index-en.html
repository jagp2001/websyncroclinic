Complete Medical Record instantly with a quick patient search

Simply enter the patient's name or ID number to open their centralized health record—from reason for visit and initial symptoms to diagnoses, exams, and prescribed treatments.

  • Search by name or ID number
  • See reason for visit and preliminary diagnosis
  • View exams uploaded at the clinic or externally
  • Display prescribed medications and lab orders
  • Access the final diagnosis with a single click
  • Records always available from any device

AI Diagnosis based on symptoms entered by the physician

The system collects the patient's symptoms from the clinical form and sends them through the OpenAI API to obtain an automated diagnostic suggestion. It does not replace the doctor, but it provides a fast and helpful reference.

  • Automatically pulls symptoms from the form
  • Sends data to OpenAI with secure integration
  • Receives a non-binding diagnostic suggestion
  • Provides clinical support, not a final diagnosis

See the suggested diagnosis on screen with a single click

After processing the symptoms, the AI-generated result appears automatically in the physician's form. This field is editable and optional, serving as a guide or reference for the clinician's final diagnosis.

  • Suggested diagnosis visible on screen
  • Optional for the physician to use
  • Optimize every visit with automated support
  • AI integrated directly into the clinical system

Attach exams with your phone from anywhere

Physicians can scan a QR code from the computer to capture photos on their phone and upload exams directly to the visit. They can also add files from a tablet or mobile device without scanning. Everything is saved in the cloud and available in the patient's history.

  • Scan a QR code to upload photos from your phone
  • Attach exams to every medical visit
  • Available from desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Exams are stored in the cloud and accessible in the chart

What is SyncroClinic and how does the medical record work?

SyncroClinic is an online clinical system that helps physicians and clinics manage patients, consultations, prescriptions, exams, and billing quickly and securely from any device with internet access.

The digital medical record is organized and easy to read. Every patient has a detailed file that displays:

  • Personal and contact information
  • Past consultations with date, diagnosis, and treatment
  • Medical prescriptions issued
  • Exam and study results
  • Preliminary and final diagnoses
  • Clinical evolution and upcoming scheduled visits

Everything is arranged in intuitive tabs and in chronological order, allowing doctors to review a patient's health status quickly and make informed decisions faster.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs in your browser. There is no software to install on your devices—just connect to the internet and log in.

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. We offer a 15-day free trial with no credit card required. During that time you can explore every feature of the platform.

What happens after the 15-day trial?

You can choose a monthly or annual membership plan that fits your needs. If you don't pick a plan, your account will remain inactive until you complete the payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards and PayPal. Choose whichever option is most convenient and secure when you subscribe.

Which plans are available?

We offer plans lasting 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, 24, and 36 months. Pick the term that fits your needs and budget—the longer the plan, the more you save.

How is the final price calculated?

Pricing is per user. Each additional user adds to the cost of the chosen plan. For example, if you choose a six-month plan for three users, the total is the monthly user price multiplied by six and by three.

Do you run special promotions?

Yes. Right now we offer a promotion that gives you one additional user for free when you purchase a plan for a single user.

What features does SyncroClinic include for clinic management?

SyncroClinic lets you maintain a complete medical history for every patient, manage schedules, record exams, issue prescriptions, coordinate future visits, and more—all from a modern platform accessible on any device.

How is each patient's medical history organized?

Each patient has a digital chart organized in tabs for personal data, consultation history, prescriptions, medical exams, and upcoming appointments. Everything is arranged chronologically to streamline the review process.

How are medical appointments scheduled?

The scheduling module lets you set each doctor's working hours in a visual grid. Patients can be booked in seconds and overlapping appointments are prevented.

What does the doctor's module include?

Physicians can view their patient list, conduct visits, write diagnoses, issue prescriptions, request exams, document clinical progress, and schedule follow-up appointments in just a few clicks.

What is the patient waiting list?

It organizes patients who are waiting to be seen. From there, physicians can call them into the visit, prioritize cases, or assign them to another professional if needed.

What tasks can the receptionist handle?

The receptionist can register patients, schedule appointments, review basic histories, and keep the daily agenda organized to support continuous, efficient care.

What does the medical exams technician module do?

It lets staff upload exam results manually or attach files from a phone using a patient-specific QR code.

How does the AI diagnostic assistant work?

The physician can click “Consult AI” to receive a suggested diagnosis based on symptoms, history, and uploaded exams. It's an assistive tool that complements the clinician's judgment.

Can I issue medical prescriptions in SyncroClinic?

Yes. You can create prescriptions with detailed instructions, save them to the patient's record, and deliver them as PDFs or printed copies directly from the system.

How do I select medical exams?

The system provides a list of common exams with filters by type and automated recommendations based on the diagnosis. You can also enter custom exams.

Can I schedule the next consultation?

Yes. When the visit ends, you can book the patient's next appointment by choosing the date and time based on the provider's availability. Everything is saved automatically.
